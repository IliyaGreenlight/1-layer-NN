rudoga- no katai koe ni reiki ga yadoru. setsuchō no me ga suxu, to hosomerarete yori surudoku togatta. shiro kinu no tebukuro ni oowareta nagai yubi ga, kunda ude wo tataiteiru. are wa, rudoga- ga saisokushiteiru toki no kuse. kore ijō, zatsudan wo shiteiru to kaminari ga ochiru na, to omoi nagara, teodoa wa derakuresu ga satsugaisareta bamen shi-n wo kaikosuru yōni me wo hosometa. sō, teodoa wa tashika ni mita. hannin rashiki jimbutsu ga derakuresu wo shisatsusuru bamen shi-n wo. gemba ni iawasete wa inai keredo, teodoa ni wa sore wo miru jutsu ga aru. dō yatte? majutsu wo tsukatte.
