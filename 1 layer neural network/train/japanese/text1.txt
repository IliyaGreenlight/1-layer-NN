rakurai no yōna surudoi daikatsu de, shiroku boyakete nigotteita teodoa no ishiki ga fujōshita. pachiri, to ichido mabataite, sarani mō ni do yukkuri to mabataki wo suru. bon-yarishiteita shikai to atama ga, jojoni rinkakusen wo toraedasu. dōyara kontōshiteita rashii. sonna koto wo omoi nagara, teodoa wa yubisaki ni wazuka na shibire wo kanjite iki wo haita. kono shibire wa, sugu ni naoru darou. keikensoku de handanshi, ushinatta mono wa nai ka atama to shintai ni maryoku wo nagasu. itatte heijōdōri no maryoku hannō ga kaettekite Teodoa wa andoshita. dekobokoshita kataku tsumetai yuka de, teodoa wa aomuke ni natteita. shōten ga aihajimeta shikai ni wa, mukibutsu to yūkibutsu ga utsurikomu. a-chi kōzō de kizukareta ishizukuri no takai tenjō, orenjishoku no hikari wo hanatsu tsuri sagerareta yō , kyūgo butai wo arawasu ryokushoku no wanshō wo tsuketa kyūgoin ga fuansō ni teodoa no kao wo nozokikondeiru. yoko wo mukeba, katsute seimei wo yadoshiteita taiku ga hitotsu. soshite, taiku no oku no kabegiwa de fukusūjin no kishidan-in ni toriosaerareteiru kuroi otoko ga hitori. teodoa ga okiagarou to jōhanshin wo ugokashita tokoro de, kyūgoin no ude ga ugoki wo seishita.
