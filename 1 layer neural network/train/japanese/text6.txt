hikui no ni myō ni hakkiri mimi ni todoku koe ni, omowazu furikaetta. soko ni ita no wa, aiiro no kami, kohakushoku no me no otoko. seikanna kaotsuki de, butchōzura wo shita dai san butai fuku taichō datta. taifuku no eri to kata ni nuitsukerareta tai Akira to kaikyū Akira ga, sō shimeshiteiru. eremiya kishidan no nishitō san kai no rōka de yobitomerare, kojinteki na enshū tsumari teawase wo mōshikomareta. totan, shinzō ga kitai to odoroki de dōki shidasu. sukoshi bakari kokyū mo hayaku natte, taion ga agaru. hō mo yurunde niya keteiru darou.
