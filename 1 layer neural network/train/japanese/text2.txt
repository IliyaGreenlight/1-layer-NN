katai yuka no ue de aomuke no mama warau teodoa wo miorosu sōsakan rudoga- no kao wa, gyakkō ni natteiru kara sō mieru no ka, soretomo ki wo ushinatteshimatta teodoa ni rakutanshite ka, hidoku kewashii mono datta. rudoga- no sonzai wo gen meshii mono ni shiteiru no wa, nani mo hyōjōsuji dake janai. shiwa naku airon ga kesareta taifuku, kubi made kichiri to tojirareta botan, hitosuji no midare mo naku ushiro ni nade katamerareta ko cha no kami. kuroku en torareta shikakui megane, surudoku hikaru tsuriagatta shinryoku no me, hakujōsō na usui kuchibiru. sōiu mono ga, rudoga- no inshō wo yori kataku kibishii mono ni shiteita. hajimete atta toki kara kawarazu o katai na, to jōkyō ni soguwanai kansō wo bon-yari to ukabe nagara, teodoa wa usui kuchibiru ga surudoi koe wo hassuru no wo kiita.
